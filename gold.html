Gold Lilies Global Services was founded in 2017 in Lagos, Nigeria as a
social impact outfit for diverse causes. It has gone ahead to be the corporate
representation for both philanthropic and commercial ventures amongst other
initiatives. Inspired by its overarching objectives to promote excellence and
humanity, the vision of the entity and all its expressions is simple –
Bringing inspiration and empowerment opportunities, to be a
lighthouse to help create better human experiences.
